Tournament Information

Bluff City Bout is the premier college tournament of the Mid South. Hosted in Memphis, this tournament brings 16 college teams together to duke it out. The last team standing will be crowned king of Memphis. The men's division tournament will occur on the weekend of November 3rd and 4th. The bid fee can be paid via PayPal or through this website after registration.
